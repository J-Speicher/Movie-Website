External Movie Review Sources

  • 1: Rotten Tomatoes

    This infamous movie review website is known for being

    overly critical of movies

  • 2: IMDb

    This website is more "mainstream" and lists reviews that most people would agree with.

  • 3: Metacritic

    Metacritic is easy to navigate through a wide selection

    and it also provides casual reviews with explanations for them.

  • 4: Roger Ebert

    It's fair to say that this website is extremely in depth, so I

    would only recommend it to those who are deeply interested in cinema.

  • 5: IGN

    IGN may be the most casual of these review websites, but it is

    entertaining and sometimes funny to scroll through.
